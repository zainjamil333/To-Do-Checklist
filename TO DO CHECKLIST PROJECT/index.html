    About To-Do Lists

    • To-do lists help you organize your day and boost productivity.
    • Break big tasks into smaller, manageable steps for better progress.
    • Prioritize tasks by category to focus on what matters most.
    • Checking off completed tasks gives a sense of achievement!
    • Review your list daily to stay on track and motivated.
